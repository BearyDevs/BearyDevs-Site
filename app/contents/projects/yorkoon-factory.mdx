## Yorkoon Factory

Yorkoon Factory is a versatile web application designed to empower users to create innovative recipes for products like cosmetics, soaps, extracts, chemicals, and more. It offers an integrated e-commerce platform where users can purchase recipes or items, providing a seamless shopping experience. Additionally, Yorkoon Factory includes a powerful CRM system that enables efficient management of quotations and customer relationships, making it a comprehensive solution for both product innovation and business operations.
[Preview](https://yorkoon-factory.vercel.app/)
