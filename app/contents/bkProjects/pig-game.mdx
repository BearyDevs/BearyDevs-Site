## Pig Game

The Pig Game is a 2D game where you have to collect coins and win. The game theme is 2D platformer and simple, two-player dice game where players take turns to roll a die as many times as they wish, accumulating points on each roll. However, if a player rolls a 1, their turn ends and they lose all the points accumulated during that turn. Players can choose to “hold” at any point during their turn to secure their accumulated points, adding them to their overall score. The objective of the game is to be the first player to reach or exceed a predetermined score, typically 100 points, without losing progress due to an unlucky roll of 1 points.
